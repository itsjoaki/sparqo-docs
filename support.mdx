## Reporting a bug

When something breaks, the fastest fix comes from a quick note on:

<Steps>
  <Step title="What you did">
    The page or action, for example "clicked Write full article on a draft".
  </Step>
  <Step title="What you expected vs what happened">
    Include the exact error text if there was one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your workspace">
    The site or domain on your account, so we can look it up.
  </Step>
</Steps>
