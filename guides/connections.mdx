Connect a tool and the agent that uses it goes from advice to action. Connections live in the Connections section of the app.

## Publishing (CMS)

Link where your content lives so articles publish with one click (or on a schedule).

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Strapi" icon="server">
    Connect with your base URL and an API token. The collection and fields are auto-detected, so you rarely type anything by hand.
  </Card>
  <Card title="GitHub" icon="github">
    For code or vibe-coded blogs: Sparqo commits a Markdown file with frontmatter to your repo, and your host redeploys.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Google Search Console

Connect Search Console to turn on the SEO flywheel. Sparqo tracks how your published articles perform and mines your real search data for gaps, emerging queries and cannibalization, then proposes the next articles to write.

## Analytics

Connect analytics (for example GA4) so Sparqo can see whether the traffic it drives actually converts, and weight its recommendations toward what moves revenue.

<Note>
  Third-party connections use managed OAuth, so you authorize once and Sparqo handles the tokens. You can disconnect any tool at any time.
</Note>
