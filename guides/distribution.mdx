The Distribution agent finds threads where your product is a genuinely helpful answer and drafts the reply. The goal is credibility, not spam: communities smell generic marketing instantly and punish it.

## The karma on-ramp

New Reddit accounts cannot promote anything without getting removed or banned. So Sparqo starts you on the on-ramp: build karma first with value-only comments that help people, and earn the right to mention your product later.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Target subreddits">
    Add the communities where your buyers actually hang out. Open the Subreddits panel to search 3,000+ real communities or let AI pick from your brand. Your tracked list lives right there, with add and remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find threads">
    The agent surfaces threads worth replying to, including Reddit threads that rank on Google (parasite SEO), and drafts a value-first reply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post manually">
    You review the draft and post it yourself from your own account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Sparqo never posts to Reddit through an API. Automated posting gets accounts banned, so posting is manual by design: you copy the approved draft and post it from your account. This protects your account.
</Warning>

## Reddit to SEO bridge

Reddit threads often rank on Google for your buyer questions. A genuinely useful reply on a ranking thread compounds: it helps a real person now, and it keeps working as an evergreen search result and an AI citation source later.
