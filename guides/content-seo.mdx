The Content & SEO agent turns your niche into ranked articles, from keyword to published post.

## Keywords

The **Keywords** tab is your keyword universe: real search queries from your niche with monthly volume, a 12 month trend, competition, cost per click, and difficulty (KD). Opportunity is scored from volume and difficulty so the winnable ones surface first.

You can build or rebuild the plan on demand (it runs live keyword data), remove keywords, and draft any of them.

<Tip>
  Prefer lower KD with decent volume. A page you can actually rank beats a high-volume term you will never crack.
</Tip>

## Drafts and articles

Writing an article is two stages, so you never waste credits:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Brief (a draft)">
    A short brief: title, the angle that wins the SERP, and an outline. Cheap and fast, so you can decide if it is worth writing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write full article">
    On demand, Sparqo writes the full long-form article (2,000+ words), structured for both readers and AI citation, with an on-brand hero image.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Articles live in the **Articles** tab, grouped into Drafts, Articles, and Published.

## Hero images

Each article gets a hero image generated in your brand's art direction (one consistent house style plus your brand colours), not generic AI stock. Set your image style and brand colours in [Settings, Brand](/guides/brand).

## Publishing

Connect a CMS and publish with one click, or schedule a live auto-publish for a chosen date. Sparqo supports connectors like Strapi and a GitHub target for code or vibe-coded blogs. See [Connections](/guides/connections).

<Note>
  Sparqo also reads Google Search Console (once connected) to find gaps, emerging queries, and cannibalization, then proposes articles that capture demand you are already close to ranking for.
</Note>
