## Visual identity

Pick one image style for every article so your blog reads as an intentional system, not a pile of random AI images. Choose a house style (editorial, isometric, 3D, line, gradient, photographic, or a painterly poster look) and set your brand colours, which anchor the palette of every generated image.

<Tip>
  You can also paint the article headline onto the hero as a serif overlay, magazine-cover style, or keep a clean illustration.
</Tip>
