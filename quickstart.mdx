title: "Quickstart"
description: "Set up Sparqo in a couple of minutes and land on real, ready-to-act work."
---

Onboarding takes a couple of minutes and ends with actual work waiting for you, not an empty dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your website">
    Sparqo does a deep read of your site (home, about, pricing, features) and builds your brand brain: what you do, your niche, your ideal customer, your voice, and your competitors. You can edit any of it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your market and language">
    Pick your primary market (the country or region that drives your keyword volumes) and your content language. You can target several markets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your keywords">
    Sparqo shows the top winnable keywords from your niche, with real monthly search volume and difficulty (KD). Toggle the ones you want, or add your own. Keywords you already cover on your blog are filtered out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to your dashboard">
    Sparqo seeds your workspace so nothing is empty:
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Keywords" icon="magnifying-glass">
        Your winnable keywords land in Content & SEO with full metrics.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Drafts" icon="file-lines">
        Three ready-to-write drafts from your top keywords (briefs only, you write them when you choose).
      </Card>
      <Card title="Subreddits" icon="reddit">
        Relevant communities are targeted in Distribution.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>
