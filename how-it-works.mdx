---
title: "How it works"
description: "The loop behind every Sparqo agent: find the work, draft it, you approve, it ships."
---

Every Sparqo agent runs the same loop, so the product feels like one team, not a pile of tools.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the work">
    Each agent scans for concrete opportunities in its area: keywords you can rank for, prompts where AI does not cite you yet, threads worth replying to. It uses your brand brain and real data (search volumes, live SERPs, AI answers) so the opportunities are grounded, not guessed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Draft it">
    The agent drafts the actual deliverable, on-brand: a full article, a Reddit reply, a plan of moves. Nothing is generic filler.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You approve">
    Drafts land in your Inbox for review, grouped by agent. You edit, approve, or skip. Nothing goes live without you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It ships">
    On approval, Sparqo publishes (to your CMS) or hands you the finished draft to post. Then it measures what happened and feeds that back into the next round.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The brand brain

At onboarding, Sparqo reads your site once and builds a brand brain: what you do, your ideal customer, your voice, your competitors, your market and language. Every agent writes from it, so Reddit, SEO and content all say a coherent thing. You can edit it anytime in Settings.

## Human in the loop, on purpose

Communities and search engines punish generic marketing instantly. Sparqo is built so every draft reads like a real, useful person wrote it, and you are always the final say. The agents do the heavy lifting; you keep the judgment.

<Note>
  Connect your own tools (CMS, Search Console, analytics) when you want an agent to act, not just advise. Until then, every draft still waits for your approval.
</Note>
