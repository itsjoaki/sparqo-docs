Sparqo is an AI Chief Marketing Officer for founders and small teams. Instead of another blank editor or a keyword report, Sparqo runs a team of specialist agents that do the actual work: writing articles, replying on Reddit, fixing your SEO. You steer, you approve, they ship.

The point is leverage: an AI CMO reads your brand, decides what to work on, and hands each job to the right specialist. Nothing goes live without your approval.

## Your team

An **AI CMO** orchestrates a squad of specialists, each with its own chat, tools and memory:

</Note>

## The loop

<Steps>
  <Step title="Steer, in chat">
    Tell your AI CMO what you want, or just ask what to do next. It reads your **Brain** (brand, voice, what it has learned) and hands the job to the right specialist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your agents draft the work">
    Each specialist drafts end to end using real data from Search Console and your analytics, on a cadence you set.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You approve, it ships">
    Finished work lands in your **Inbox**, grouped by agent. One tap to approve and it publishes. Nothing goes out without your OK.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Sparqo keeps a human in the loop by design. Communities and search engines punish generic marketing, so every draft is meant to read like a real, useful person wrote it. You are the final say.
