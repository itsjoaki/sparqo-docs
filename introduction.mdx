title: "What is Sparqo?"
description: "An AI CMO for founders: growth agents that find the work and draft it for your approval."

Sparqo is an AI Chief Marketing Officer for founders and small teams. Instead of another blank editor or keyword report, Sparqo runs a team of specialist agents that spot growth opportunities and draft the actual work. You review it, you approve it, they ship it.

The point is leverage: you set the direction, the agents handle the execution, and nothing goes live without your approval.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your site">
    Sparqo reads your website once and builds a brand brain: what you do, your ICP, your voice, competitors, and your market.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It finds the work">
    Each agent turns that into concrete opportunities: keywords you can rank for, prompts you are missing citations on, threads worth replying to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You approve, it ships">
    The agent drafts each piece. You review and approve. Sparqo publishes or hands you the finished draft.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Sparqo keeps a human in the loop by design. Communities and search engines punish generic marketing, so every draft is meant to read like a real, useful person wrote it. You are the final say.
</Note>
